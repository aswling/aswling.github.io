Hi! I'm Alex.

I'm a senior at NYU’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study pursuing an individualized major focused in design, art history, and marketing. Currently, I'm the graphic designer of The Literacy Review Vol. 16 (set for publication in Spring 2018) and the design intern at Amino. I'm interested in interdisciplinary approaches to visual forms and the formation of art audiences. In my free time, I draw, make comics, and code. I also recently started tinkering with Arduino and physical computing. I have professional experience in digital and print design, social media design, illustration, and making creative content. Feel free to get in touch at alexswling@gmail.com!
