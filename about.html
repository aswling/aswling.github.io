Hey, I'm Alex.

I'm a Chinese-American illustrator, comics maker, and designer based in NYC. I'm interested in the boundaries of illustration, design, and photo/video, and usually combine these mediums to create self-published work in risograph. Creatively, I often explore themes of belonging, growth, and connection.

Currently, I'm a Brand Designer at Etsy. Selected clients include Asian American Arts Alliance (A4), Google, Starbucks, Chase, Gympass, Roku, JetBlue, Sony, and the International Rescue Committee. In my spare time, you can find me biking along the Hudson River and climbing rocks. (I also made this site from scratch, so welcome to any tips and feedback ✌)
