Hi! I'm Alex and I'm a senior at NYU’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study pursuing an individualized major focused on design, art history, and marketing. Currently, I'm the graphic designer of The Literacy Review and recently I interned at Amino on the design team. I'm interested in interdisciplinary approaches to visual forms and the formation of art audiences. I also make comics, web-based projects, and doodle. Feel free to get in touch at aswling@nyu.edu!
