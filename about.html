Hi! I'm Alex.

I currently work at Big Spaceship as an Associate Designer. My main client is Starbucks, and I've also worked with brands including Roku, JetBlue, Google Stadia, Sony, and the International Rescue Committee.

From designing motion graphics for a giant coffee cup LED screen, to art directing an annual publication that showcases writing from ESOL students, I'm driven by the ways creativity brings people together. I'm interested in how design can enhance community and expand collaborative possibilities.

Outside of work, you can find me making comics, slaving over a hot risograph machine, and trying to code a coherent portfolio site (yes, this site was coded from scratch because...why not).
