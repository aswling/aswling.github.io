Everything In Between

Skills: Webcomic, illustration web design, web development

Tools: Photoshop, HTML/CSS, PHP

Everything In Between is an ongoing webcomic about Holyk, a psychic and paranormal investigator who resolves conflicts between the supernatural and human worlds. They've been on the job for awhile so little fazes them. However, when a student named Jamie believes her friend is being haunted by a human who mutated into a malicious spirit, Holyk realizes they may have discovered something more sinister than they've ever seen before.
