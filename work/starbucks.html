Starbucks

At Big Spaceship, Starbucks is my primary account. As the primary designer on the team, my responsibilities include everything from digital design and illustration, to post-production and storyboarding.

Fall (2019)

Big Spaceship was briefed to create a campaign that announced the return of Starbucks' Pumpkin Spice Latte. The iconic drink is basically synonymous with fall, and since the team had just wrapped up the Summer campaign, we proposed designing a PSL machine that "turned summer into fall." This ultimately led to the "Fallifier" Facebook livestream. viewers voted on summer items to send through a giant PSL cup to "Fallify." I helped design the look of the machinery, and I led the design and animation of all of the motion graphics and LED screen imagery used throughout the livestream. The event drew 247K unique viewers, and user commentary engagement was more than double that of the previous year's Holiday livestream.

Nitro (2019)

To announce the launch of Nitro Cold Brew, Big Spaceship and Starbucks leaned into the "Whoa!" feeling of the energizing drink. I was responsible for post-production (videoediting, photoediting, color correction, etc), storyboarding pre-production comps, and designing and packaging banners to be produced by our media partner. Part of our campaign was featured in AdWeek.
