The Amp

The Amp is an online magazine from the Asian American Arts Alliance (A4) that celebrates the vibrant, multifaceted, and rich AAPI cultural community in NYC and beyond. To celebrate one year of the publication, A4 put together a special risograph-printed issue in October 2023. I had the opportunity to contribute an illustrated timeline spread of A4's history.

The magazine was edited by Shannon Lee, designed by Helen Chen, and printed by Lucky Risograph.
