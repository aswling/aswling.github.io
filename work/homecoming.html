Homecoming (回家)

Skills: Comic, illustration, print design

Tools: Photoshop, InDesign

Created for Visual Culture and the Politics of Images at NYU Gallatin in Spring 2017. Special thanks to Professor Lauren Walsh for her guidance throughout the semester. The full comic can be read here.

Homecoming seeks to reveal the conflicts within Chinese-American identity that often go unsaid. Usually tied to “model minority” myths and familial expectations that turn into internalized pressure, the characters of Homecoming struggle to confront their moments of vulnerability and doubt. The protagonist serves as an observer as they exchange text messages with their friend and sister, acting as the common thread between their stories.
