Everything In Between is a webcomic pitch about Holyk, a psychic and paranormal investigator who resolves conflicts between the supernatural and human worlds. They've been on the job for awhile so very little fazes them. However, when a student named Jamie believes her friend is being haunted by a human who mutated into a malicious spirit, Holyk realizes they may have discovered something more sinister than they've ever seen before.

This poster was created for Design Studio at NYU in Fall 2017. The exercise was to make a poster based on a specific stance on gun violence in the United States. I decided to make an infographic that explores the correlation between gun control and suicide, specifically how the gun violence discussion tends to focus on shootings, but often overlooks ways to combat America's gun suicide rate.
