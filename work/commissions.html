Commissions

Lunar New Year for Crystal Creations: Crystal Creations wanted designs for a banner and posters to commemorate New Year festivities at Tanglin Mall in Singapore. The banner was going to serve as a backdrop for live cultural performances and the posters promoted the month-long events. Working with the client, we decided on an art direction that drew from traditional Chinese visual motifs (e.g. red and gold, lanterns, face-changing masks) with a contemporary style that was festive and playful.
