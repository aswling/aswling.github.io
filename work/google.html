Google

Google (Maps and Local Guides) is one of the accounts I’ve worked on at Big Spaceship. My responsibilities include everything from graphic design and illustration to motion graphics and UI animation.

Maps Holiday (2020)

With the holidays being a bit different in 2020, we wanted to show the different ways people could use Google Maps to safely navigate the season. Working with the creative team, I led and owned the design for design on Maps holiday. We created stop motion videos to show ways to support local businesses, interactive IG stories, and sent physical holidays cards to Maps partners.
